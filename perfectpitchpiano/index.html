Perfect Pitch Piano is an iPad game for learning to play piano by ear! It plays a melody and you play it back. It's simple and fun. You'll learn to:
  • Play the piano (even if you've never played before)
  • Improve your ear (helps all aspects of music)
  • Learn the building blocks of music (like intervals)
  • Learn classic songs like Amazing Grace, Twinkle Twinkle Little Star and Mary Had a Little Lamb
